WELCOME

Doctors and health workers are facing lots of difficulties while taking care of the affected individuals, as they have to take all the necessary precautions both to protect themselves and prevent any further outbreak The recent outbreak of Covid-19 and the lack of a working vaccine is one of the global emergencies the world is facing during this time period These issues can be addressed by implementing robotic devices that can assist the healthcare workers in the fight against the growing pandemic The project mainly aims to defend the spread of viruses like coronavirus, by taking safe measures to analyse the health condition of patients and help them with necessary medication
